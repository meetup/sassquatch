Divided List

List items in dividedList expect block-level elements, like p or img. dividedList and paddedList usually live inside a docSection wrapper.

  • Eggs

  • Milk

  • Beer

<div class="docBox">
        <div class="docSection">
            <ul class="dividedList">
                <li><p>Eggs</p></li>
                <li><p>Milk</p></li>
                <li><p>Beer</p></li>
            </ul>
        </div>
    </div>

Padded List

The paddedList pattern is the same as dividedList, but without borders.

  • Eggs

  • Milk

  • Beer

<div class="docBox">
        <div class="docSection">
            <ul class="paddedList">
                <li><p>Eggs</p></li>
                <li><p>Milk</p></li>
                <li><p>Beer</p></li>
            </ul>
        </div>
    </div>

Section List

A type of dividedList pattern that directly divides a docBox without a docSection element.

  • Eggs

  • Milk

  • Beer

<div class="docBox">
        <ul class="dividedList">
            <li><p>Eggs</p></li>
            <li><p>Milk</p></li>
            <li><p>Beer</p></li>
        </ul>
    </div>
